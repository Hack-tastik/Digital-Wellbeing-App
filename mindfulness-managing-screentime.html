Tips for Managing Screen Time

Excessive screen time can negatively impact your mental and physical health. Here are some strategies to help you manage your screen time effectively:

  • Set Screen Time Limits: Use apps or phone settings to limit screen time for social media, apps, and gaming.
  • Take Regular Breaks: Practice the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, take a 20-second break and look at something 20 feet away.
  • Mindful Use of Technology: Before picking up your phone or opening an app, ask yourself if itâ€™s truly necessary.
  • Engage in Non-Digital Activities: Take up hobbies such as reading, exercising, or spending time outdoors to balance your screen time.
  • Practice Digital Detox: Set aside tech-free hours or days where you disconnect from all digital devices to recharge.

By being mindful of your screen time, you can reduce stress and promote a healthier, more balanced lifestyle.
